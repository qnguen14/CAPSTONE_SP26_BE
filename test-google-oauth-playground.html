Alternative: Manual Test with PowerShell

After getting your token, run this in PowerShell:

$token = "YOUR_GOOGLE_TOKEN_HERE" $body = @{ googleToken = $token } | ConvertTo-Json Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:5057/api/v1/google-login" -Method POST -Body $body -ContentType "application/json"
